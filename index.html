Abstract

This is the ontology documentation for the SSHOC-NL Knowledge Graph, available at https://kg.odissei.nl/.

Introduction back to ToC

In essence, the SSHOC-NL Ontology translates the complex, nuanced world of scholarly information into a structured model, making it more accessible and useful for both human users and automated systems.
